# Legal Questions

These questions require a healthcare attorney before production.

## HIPAA and role analysis

- Is the company a covered entity, business associate, subcontractor, or direct-to-consumer health app outside HIPAA for the planned launch?
- If the app connects to providers or health systems, when is a business associate agreement required?
- If users independently authorize data import, what obligations attach to received data?
- What are the minimum required policies before storing real protected health information?

## Consumer health privacy

- Does the FTC Health Breach Notification Rule apply?
- Which state consumer health data laws apply based on user location and business practices?
- What consent language is required for importing, storing, using, and deleting health data?
- What disclosures are required for AI-assisted organization of health data?

## FHIR/source integrations

- What terms apply to Epic/MyChart, Apple Health, Health Connect, and other portal integrations?
- Are there restrictions on storing, caching, or redisplaying imported data?
- What source branding or user-facing notices are required?
- What app registration, certification, or review is required before production access?

## AI and clinical boundaries

- How should the product disclaim non-diagnostic behavior without weakening user trust?
- What review is needed to ensure AI output is administrative organization rather than medical advice?
- What records must be retained to audit AI proposals and patient confirmations?

## Data rights

- What export, deletion, correction, and retention rights must be supported?
- How should raw source records be deleted if the patient requests deletion?
- How should confirmed current-profile corrections be handled when they conflict with imported source data?
